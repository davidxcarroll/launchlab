Sales Success In Two Economic Eras

Westgate Resort, Park City, Utah

In the boom years we set a Utah state record for most sales in a single day. In 2011 and 2012 we were brought in to re-sell condo deals that had evaporated in the downturn.

2006 — 2007
  • Recruited and trained sales team, admin staff and 10 sales agents
  • Devised national strategic marketing plan to generate qualified leads
  • 2006 pre-sale launch generated $65M
  • $100M first year sales
2008 — 2009
  • In 2008 real estate downturn the mortgage bank pulled condo financing and sales dropped out before lodge construction was complete
  • Following a failed auction attempt, developer construction loan was past due
  • Retained to kickstart an onsite sales effort
  • We closed 43% of all condos sold at Canyon Resort
2010
  • Sales met and exceeded bank’s construction loan requirements
  • Sold another 50 condos for 48% of all Canyons Resort unit sales
  • Bank construction loan successfully repaid
